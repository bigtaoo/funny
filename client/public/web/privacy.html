This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you play Nivara. We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

The data controller is Tao Wang, reachable at support@gamestao.com.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, our Merchant of Record. Paddle collects and stores your payment details (card number, billing address) directly — we never see or store full card data. Paddle's privacy policy applies to payment data: paddle.com/legal/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Paddle — payment processing (see §3)
  • Infrastructure providers — cloud hosting (servers located in the EU/EEA where possible)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your session token and game settings. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. A consent dialog is shown on first launch.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Transaction records are kept for 7 years to comply with EU accounting law. You may request deletion of your account at any time (see §7).

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR you have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccurate data, erase your data ("right to be forgotten"), restrict or object to processing, and data portability. To exercise any right, email support@gamestao.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures including HTTPS, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via in-game notice or email.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or GDPR requests: support@gamestao.com.
