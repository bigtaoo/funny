The war for the notebook
is turn-based.

Nivara — also known as Notebook Wars — is a hand-drawn, turn-based strategy game fought in the margins of a school notebook. Command doodled armies, outmaneuver your rival lane by lane, and carry your progress across a solo campaign, ranked duels, and season-long kingdom warfare.

What you play

One game, three ways to play — all sharing the same roster of heroes and cosmetics.

Story Campaign

A single-player journey through hand-drawn chapters. Learn the lane-combat system, unlock heroes, and follow the story of two rival notebooks.

Ranked Duels (PvP)

Head-to-head, deterministic turn-based battles. Climb a seasonal ladder, earn ranking rewards, and prove your loadout against real opponents.

Kingdom Seasons

A large-scale strategy layer: gather resources, build a home city, march on rivals, and take part in sieges across a shared seasonal map.

Heroes & Cosmetics

Collect and equip a roster of characters, customize them with stationery-themed skins, and gear up with equipment you earn or unlock.

Cross-platform

Play instantly in any modern web browser, or on iOS and Android. Your account and progress follow you across devices.

Fair by design

Battles are server-authoritative and deterministic. Loot-box odds and pity guarantees are disclosed in-game — no hidden mechanics.
